Employee Manhours

How to enter Employee Manhours?

1.       Go to Desktop and Click on the following icon to login to LAS Database

        Following Login screen will appear after selecting the LAS Database icon

2.       Enter Username and Password and press Submit button.

Username is the employee number (0012, 0007, etc ).

When you login to the system for the first time, it will request for a new Password for your username. Employee need to remember the password and not to be told to anyone else. One should not use the commonly used passwords like “las” or the initials of employee.

 

3.       After entering Username and password correctly, following webpage will appear on the computer screen.

 

4.       Select Manhour tab =>Employee Manhours(Timesheet Entry) to open the Timesheet. Select Week Start Date by clicking on the right white column.

 

5.       Select Week Start date to enter manhours. Click first on the right side of the “select week start date” to show the month calender and then select start day of the week by clicking any date on the week.

6.       Enter the manhours after selecting project, department and activity.

 

7.       After entering the manhours, click on the button “Save” to save the manhours in the database.

 

8.       To come out of the program without saving the entered manhours, click on “Quit” button.

 

9.       Once all the entries are over for a week, the timesheet need to be submitted by clicking “Submit for Approval” button. Once submitted, the manhours for that week cannot be changed the employee.

 

10.   If manager sends back the timesheet without approving then employee will be able to modify the timesheet and submit it for approval again.
